## üéì Best Practices

### **Before You Scan**
1. **Define clear acquisition criteria** (don't scan everything)
2. **Set realistic parameters** (too broad = noise, too narrow = missed opportunities)
3. **Budget for comprehensive scans** (better data = better decisions)
4. **Plan your follow-up process** (how will you engage targets?)

### **During Execution**
1. **Monitor progress** (check for data quality issues)
2. **Review partial results** (adjust if needed)
3. **Track costs** (stay within budget)

### **After Completion**
1. **Prioritize targets** (focus on top 10-15 first)
2. **Create engagement plan** (personalized outreach)
3. **Track pipeline** (from scan ‚Üí conversation ‚Üí LOI ‚Üí close)
4. **Learn and iterate** (refine criteria for next scan)
